Common questions

Where is my data stored?

On your device, in a local database. Lens has no cloud backend for your financial data. If you delete the app without backing up, the data is gone. See the Privacy Policy for the architecture details.

How do I back up my data?

Settings → Data & backup → Export. Choose a location (iCloud Drive recommended). The file is your snapshot. To restore, install Lens on a new device and use Settings → Data & backup → Restore.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple, so cancellation happens in your Apple ID settings, not in Lens. iPhone: Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Lens Pro → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

I bought Lifetime. Why does the app still show the upgrade prompt?

Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom. Apple sometimes takes a moment to propagate the entitlement, especially if you switched devices. If it still doesn't unlock after 5 minutes, email us with your Apple ID purchase receipt.

How does the AI feature work?

Ask AI uses your own OpenRouter API key (BYOK). You bring the key from openrouter.ai, paste it into Settings, and pick which model you want to use. Your prompts go directly from your device to OpenRouter, never through any Lens server.

How does receipt scanning work?

Two paths. The first uses on-device OCR built into iOS, no internet required. The second uses your OpenRouter API key for AI parsing on more complex receipts. Which one runs depends on your settings.

Why does Lens not connect to my bank?

Bank Sync is a planned add-on, not yet live in v1. When it ships, it will be optional and clearly labelled. For now, transactions are added manually or by importing a CSV file from your bank.

I found a bug. What do I do?

Email hello@edniroya.com with what you were doing, what you expected, and what actually happened. A screenshot helps. We read every message and reply within a few days.

Where can I read the privacy policy?

privacy.html. Short version: your financial data never leaves your device.
